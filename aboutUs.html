    • Inspired by a desire to raise awareness about the immediate and long- term consequences of our choices, TDT was created. We believe in the power of the present moment and aim to remind everyone that the decisions made today can shape our tomorrow in ways we might not anticipate.

    Our core values center on spreading the message from this mission and reminding everyone of their choices, whether they like it or not.
